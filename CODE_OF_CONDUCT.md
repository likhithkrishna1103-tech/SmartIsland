# Code Of Conduct

Smart Island should be a useful, respectful, and welcoming open-source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still asking for clarity.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, the design, and the code.
- Help keep public discussions free of private data and sensitive logs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or exclusionary language.
- Sharing someone else's private information without permission.
- Posting secrets, tokens, private notification content, or exploit details in public issues.
- Repeated spam, off-topic promotion, or disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not follow this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent restrictions from the project.

If you see a problem, report it through the repository's moderation or security channels.
